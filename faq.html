FAQ

Ask a different question

1. What are the fees for the programs offered at New Pathway Institute?

We understand that the cost of education is an important consideration. Our fees vary depending on the program you choose. For detailed information on tuition fees for each course, please visit our institute or contact our admissions team at 01 3643262 and 09 252511414 for a personalized fee structure.

2. What is the schedule for classes?

Class schedules vary depending on the program and level. We offer flexible timings, including morning, afternoon, and evening classes, to accommodate the different needs of our students. You can inquire about the specific schedule for your course of interest by calling 01 3643262 and 09 252511414.

3. How do I register for a course?

To register for a course, simply visit our institute or contact our admissions team for guidance. You will need to fill out an application form and provide necessary documentation. Our team will assist you with the entire registration process.

4. Can I take multiple courses at the same time?

Yes, depending on your schedule and course availability, you may choose to enroll in multiple courses. We recommend speaking with an academic advisor to ensure that the course load is manageable for you.

5. What are the class sizes?

We strive to maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention and effective learning. The average class size ranges from 5 to 20 students, depending on the program.

6. How do I contact the admissions office for further inquiries?

You can reach our admissions office by phone at 01 3643262 and 09 252511414. We are happy to assist you with any additional questions.
